Follow the rules

List of all Minecraft and Discord server rules.

Ignorance of the rules is no excuse!

Minecraft server

  1. Respect Others: Treat all players with respect, kindness, and courtesy. No bullying, harassment, or offensive language is allowed.
  2. No Cheating or Hacking: Any use of cheats, hacks, or exploiting glitches is strictly prohibited. Play fair and have fun.
  3. Keep the Chat Clean: Use appropriate language and refrain from spamming, advertising, or excessive use of caps.
  4. Respect the Staff: Listen to and follow the instructions of the server staff. They are here to ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience for everyone.
  5. Report Issues: If you encounter any problems or witness rule violations, report them to the server staff promptly.
  6. No Impersonation: Do not impersonate server staff or other players.
  7. Once Frozen do not leave the server or you will be punished.
  8. Have Fun: Most importantly, have fun and enjoy your time on the server!

Practice Mode

  1. PvP Rules: PvP (Player versus Player) is allowed, but players must engage in fair 1v1 combat. No free hitting, spawn camping, or unfair advantages during PvP.
  2. No FreeHitting! Basically do not interfere a ongoing 1v1, if caught you will be banned. (Exception is CPVP)
  3. Do not advance a user without a confirmed 1v1.
  4. Do not use any unfair advantage mods, such as Walky Crystal Optimizer, etc.
  5. Once Frozen do not leave the server or you will be punished.

Lifesteal Mode

  1. TPA Traps: This is completely allowed! If this happens to you then we cannot do anything!
  2. Alt Steal: You are not allowed to alt farm for hearts.
  3. Freeze: Once Frozen do not leave the server or you will be punished.
  4. Scamming: Scamming is completely allowed, trust who you want to trust.
  5. Xray: Xray is not allowed, if caught will be punished

Discord server

  1. Be respectful: Treat fellow players with respect, avoiding rudeness, offensive behavior, or personal attacks.
  2. Use the right channels: Post content and discussions in the appropriate channels to keep conversations organized and focused on PvP-related topics.
  3. No excessive caps or chat flooding: Avoid using excessive capital letters or spamming the chat with repetitive messages.
  4. No advertising: Do not promote or advertise other Minecraft servers, websites, or communities without permission from the server administrators.
  5. No offensive language or behavior: Avoid using offensive, discriminatory, or derogatory language that may create an unwelcoming environment for other players.
  6. Protect privacy: Respect the privacy of other players and refrain from sharing personal information without proper consent.
  7. Follow server-specific rules: Adhere to any additional rules or guidelines set by the server administrators to ensure fair and enjoyable PvP gameplay for everyone.
  8. Report issues: If you encounter any rule violations, hacking, or other issues related to PvP gameplay, promptly report them to the server moderators or administrators for action.

If you have read the rules, you can go and enjoy the fun on our server!
