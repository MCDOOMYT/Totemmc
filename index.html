Who we are?

TotemmcMC was created by the previous owners of WoolMC/Atexmc and MasterMc and is based in Asia. It has a variety of PvP types so that everyone can practice, have fun, and duel each other.

Practice

In our practice mode, you are teleported into an arena where you can find people to pvp with and defeat! You can also look at top stats and rtp players to have a good crystal pvp 1v1 match.

  • Sword PvP
  • Crystal PvP
  • Pot PvP
  • Duels
  • and more

Lifesteal

In our lifesteal mode, you are sent into a spawn area where you you can RTP into the wild and build a base and steal others hearts and enjoy a lot more features which can be accessed at the spawn!

  • Custom Spawn
  • Warp Arena
  • SHOP
  • COINSHOP
  • and more

Battle Royale

A fast-paced survival game where crafting and combat lead to being the last player standing

  • 30+ Custom Items
  • 5+ Custom Arenas
  • Cosmetics, Effects
  • and more

Server FAQs.

Here are some Server FAQs that you might find useful!

Do you have a Forums Page?
No. We don't have but we are working on it
What Versions does the server support?
The Server lets you join with any versions stating from 1.16.5 to the latest available Minecraft Version.
Where do I go for help if I have a problem?
You can join our Discord server and open a ticket there to get help with whatever problem you're having.
Are we allowed to make suggestions for features? Will they ever be accepted?
Yes, you are allowed to make suggestions or feature additions, but we may reject some of them if we are opposed to them, and if we aren't, you're probably lucky.
